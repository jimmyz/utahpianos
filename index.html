About us

Process

Select models with historical prevalence, brand recognition, natural beauty, market demand, significant potential and the possibility of great value for our customers.


Thorough and detailed cleaning.


Assess piano performance and inspect condition of key components. Order, replace, repair, or refurbish parts as needed.


Touch up or refinish surface and reassemble


Tune and prepare for delivery.



We work to make every piano we recondition into a beautiful instrument and family treasure. Thanks for letting us serve you for over 45 years.
